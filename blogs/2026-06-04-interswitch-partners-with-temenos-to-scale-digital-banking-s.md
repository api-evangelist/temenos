Temenos, a banking technology leader, has partnered with Interswitch, a major African payments and commerce company, to deliver digital banking services across the continent. Interswitch will use Temenos's cloud-native platform for core banking, payments, and wealth management to serve financial institutions in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and other African markets. This collaboration expands Temenos's presence in Africa while enabling Interswitch to scale its banking offerings beyond its traditional payments business.
